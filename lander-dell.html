Dell Technologies Rewards

Role
  • UI Designer
Timeframe
  • 3 weeks
Industry
  • Technology
  • Personal PC

Introduction

Dell Rewards needed a high-converting landing page that clearly communicated program benefits and drove sign-ups. I designed a user-first experience that kept the brand’s visual integrity while simplifying the enrollment decision.

01The Challenge

Translate a feature-rich rewards program into a simple, compelling story. The design needed to:

  • Simplify complexity: Break down how the program works into easy, scannable steps.
  • Drive action: Keep the enrollment CTA visible and persuasive without feeling pushy.
  • Preserve brand trust: Use Dell’s blue palette, typography, and design system for familiarity.

02Design Approach

A direct, benefit-led structure with clear hierarchy. I crafted the headline "Get The Tech You Want Now With a Dell Rewards Account!" to promise immediacy and value.

The design integrates Dell’s signature blue with neutral accents, ensuring trust and brand alignment. Visual modules make key benefits stand out without overwhelming users.

03The Process

The page was structured into clear, goal-focused sections:

  • How Dell Rewards Works: Step-by-step clarity on joining, earning, and redeeming.
  • Earning Points: Simple visuals showing how everyday purchases accumulate value.
  • Exclusive Offers: Perks and bonuses framed to feel members-only.
  • FAQ: Preemptively answering objections to reduce sign-up hesitation.

Strategic CTA placement keeps enrollment top-of-mind, with styling aligned to Dell’s design language. The layout is fully responsive for a consistent mobile and desktop experience.

04Final Outcome

The redesigned landing page drove measurable growth in enrollments and engagement:

  • Enrollment increased by 18% in the first month.
  • Bounce rate decreased by 10% as users explored program details longer.
  • Conversion improved from 12% to 18% with clearer copy and optimized CTA placement.

This project demonstrates how aligning brand design with a simplified, user-focused flow can boost conversions and strengthen customer trust.
