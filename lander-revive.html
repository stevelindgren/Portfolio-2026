Revive Credit

Role
  • UI Designer
  • Front-End Dev
Timeframe
  • 3 weeks
Industry
  • Catalog Card
  • Subprime Card

Introduction

Revive Credit is designed for people rebuilding credit who want a fair chance without hidden barriers. I created a landing page that delivers a message of possibility while keeping the process simple, clear, and trustworthy.

01The Challenge

Reach an audience often excluded from traditional credit offers and build enough trust for them to apply. The key benefits—no credit checks, low monthly payments, buy now/pay later—had to be front and center without overwhelming or overpromising.

02Design Approach

Messaging: Lead with a hopeful but clear headline and reinforce it with straightforward benefit statements.

Visual Hierarchy: Order content so visitors see the most persuasive benefits immediately, followed by steps to apply, and testimonials to build confidence.

Design System: Orange for energy and optimism, blue for stability and trust. Responsive layout ensures clarity and consistency on all devices.

03The Process

  • Wireframing & Prototyping: Sketched layouts prioritizing clarity and low cognitive load.
  • User Testing: Collected feedback to confirm the headline, steps, and testimonials resonated with the target audience.
  • A/B Testing: Experimented with variations; the winning version combined the bold headline with simplified steps and genuine testimonials.

04Final Outcome

  • 25% increase in applications in the first month.
  • Testimonials improved user trust and time on page.
  • Step-by-step section reduced abandonment in the application process.

The design balances hope and realism, guiding users to see Revive Credit as a credible path to rebuilding their financial future.
